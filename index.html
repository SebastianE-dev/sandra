Go Freelance

Go Freelance has been written with you in mind. 10 years of freelance knowledge in one handy Kindle-friendly ebook. It covers everything from getting started, landing work, dealing with clients and a lot more.

Who Is It For?

Go Freelance has been written for digital and print creators - from designers and illustrators to front-end and back-end developers.

If you've spent (or plan on spending) any time working in the digital creative industry then it should be a pretty good fit for you.

What’s Covered

Go Freelance covers everything from helping with your initial decision and helping you to get set up to dealing with finances, finding work and everything else you might need to think about.

It’s the book that I wished I had all those years ago when I started my freelance career.

What Do I Get?

You get 10 years of freelance experience packed into an easy to follow PDF ebook

(which works nicely on Amazon Kindle). The book is broken down into useful sections so that you can read it cover to cover or dip in and out as you need to get the information you want.

Go Freelance is an easy and enjoyable read, a great resource for people thinking about freelancing. I made the move 6 months ago and this book gave me confirmation on the things I was doing right and also had some new and useful tips. Highly recommended.
